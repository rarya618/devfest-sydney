## Theme

> "Build, Secure, Scale: Developers and Builders in the Agentic Era"

The role of the professional developer is evolving — from writing code to reviewing it.

## Tracks

### 1. Developer Track
Technical sessions for professional engineers.

Topics: Agentic app development, Gemini API, Flutter, Firebase, Android, Google Cloud

### 2. Builder Track
For non-traditional builders — product managers, designers, and founders.

Topics: Prototyping with AI, automation, no-code and low-code tooling

### 3. Workshops Track
Hands-on sessions where attendees build alongside the speaker. Open to any topic or audience, from either the Developer or Builder track.

### 4. Builder Showcase
Mid-afternoon session where attendees present 5-minute demos, with audience voting.

## Special Features
